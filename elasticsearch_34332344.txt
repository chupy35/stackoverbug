<p>Is it possible to retrieve the hit field name using the following query?</p>&#xA;&#xA;<pre><code>"query": {&#xA;  "query_string": {&#xA;     "query": "the quick brown fox"&#xA;  }&#xA;}&#xA;</code></pre>&#xA;&#xA;<p>The documentation says</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>"Defaults to the index.query.default_field index settings, which in turn defaults to _all."</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>But what I need is the correct hit field name.</p>&#xA;
