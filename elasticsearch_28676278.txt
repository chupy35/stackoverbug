Elasticsearch 'between' queries
<p>How can I do a 'between' query in Elasticsearch?</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Basically, I want this.</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Find TermA only if it exists between Phrase1 and Phrase2.</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Phrase1 --- TermA --- Phrase2  => Match</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>A specific example:</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Source text :  "The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog."</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>I want to match, in order.</p>&#xA;&#xA;<ul>&#xA;<li>PhraseA = "The quick brown"</li>&#xA;<li>Term = "jumped"</li>&#xA;<li>PhraseB = "lazy dog"</li>&#xA;</ul>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Any guidance would be helpful.</p>&#xA;
