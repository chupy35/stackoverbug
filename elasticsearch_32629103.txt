<p>If you define your date field as string then Elasticsearch will break it up into individual terms:</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p><code>2015-09-17 17:31:52.3691732</code>&#xA;will become</p>&#xA;&#xA;<pre><code>2015&#xA;09&#xA;17&#xA;17&#xA;31&#xA;52.3691732&#xA;</code></pre>&#xA;&#xA;<p>When you sort descending then <code>52.3691732</code> will appear first (due to the leading <code>5</code>).</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>if you want to sort this field as a date - you'll need to define it as a <strong>date</strong> in your mapping.</p>&#xA;
