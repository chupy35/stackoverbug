How to specify fields which should not be indexed in ElasticSearch
<p>How do I specify the fields in ElasticSearch which I <em>do not</em> want to be indexed? I read <a href="https://stackoverflow.com/questions/13626617/specify-which-fields-are-indexed-in-elasticsearch">this SO question</a> but I didn't understand its answers.</p>&#xA;
<p>Your mapping should be something like this:</p>&#xA;&#xA;<pre><code>"index_name": {&#xA;  "mappings": {&#xA;    "type_name": {&#xA;      "mappings": {&#xA;        "properties": {&#xA;          "unsearchable_field_name": {&#xA;            "index": "no"&#xA;          }&#xA;        }&#xA;      }&#xA;    }&#xA;  }&#xA;}&#xA;</code></pre>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Notice that <code>unsearchable_field_name</code> still may to be stored (depends on your dynamic mapping config, take a look into <a href="https://www.elastic.co/guide/en/elasticsearch/guide/current/dynamic-mapping.html" rel="nofollow">Dynamic Mapping</a>), but by setting <code>index: no</code>, Elasticsearch ignores this field on search requests.</p>&#xA;
