DELETE all documents of a type in ElasticSearch 2.x
<p>I've updated ElasticSearch to 2.1 version.</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Before of that, I deleted every document in a type using:</p>&#xA;&#xA;<pre><code>curl -XDELETE '&lt;server_node&gt;:&lt;port&gt;/&lt;index&gt;/&lt;type&gt;' -i&#xA;curl -XDELETE 'http://ESNode01:9201/living/inputs' -i&#xA;</code></pre>&#xA;&#xA;<p>From now then, this is the response:</p>&#xA;&#xA;<blockquote>&#xA;  <p>HTTP/1.1 400 Bad Request Content-Type: text/plain; charset=UTF-8 </p>&#xA;  &#xA;  <p>Content-Length: 61</p>&#xA;  &#xA;  <p>No handler found for uri [/living/inputs] and method [DELETE]</p>&#xA;</blockquote>&#xA;&#xA;<p>What am I doing wrong?</p>&#xA;
<p>Deleting a mapping type is <a href="https://www.elastic.co/guide/en/elasticsearch/reference/current/breaking_20_mapping_changes.html#_types_may_no_longer_be_deleted">not supported anymore</a> since 2.0.</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>If you just need to delete the documents, then you may use the <a href="https://www.elastic.co/guide/en/elasticsearch/plugins/current/plugins-delete-by-query.html">delete by query plugin</a>, otherwise you should create a new index without the mapping you want to delete and reindex your data.</p>&#xA;
