dump ended with error (set phase) => Error: read ECONNRESET
<p>I am using elasticdump to dump data from local machine to the server. But my dumps always ended with this error:</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>...</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Tue, 20 Oct 2015 22:56:35 GMT | sent 100 objects to destination elasticsearch, wrote 100</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Tue, 20 Oct 2015 22:56:35 GMT | got 100 objects from source elasticsearch (offset: 21200)</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Tue, 20 Oct 2015 22:56:36 GMT | Error Emitted => read ECONNRESET</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Tue, 20 Oct 2015 22:56:36 GMT | Total Writes: 21200</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Tue, 20 Oct 2015 22:56:36 GMT | dump ended with error (set phase)  => Error: read ECONNRESET</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>...</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>How should I solve this problem?&#xA;Is there a better way to dump data from local machine to the server? Thanks in advance!</p>&#xA;
<p>It sounds like your issue is being caused by the elasticdump opening too many sockets to your elasticsearch cluster. You can use the <code>--maxSockets</code> option to limit the number of sockets opened.</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>For example:</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p><code>$ elasticdump --input http://192.168.2.222:9200/index1 --output http://192.168.2.222:9200/index2 --type=data --maxSockets=5</code></p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>You can find a detailed explanation of the issue here:&#xA;<a href="https://github.com/taskrabbit/elasticsearch-dump/issues/98" rel="noreferrer">https://github.com/taskrabbit/elasticsearch-dump/issues/98</a></p>&#xA;
