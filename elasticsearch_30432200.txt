NGramPhraseQuery using Elasticsearch
<p>How can I perform an<a href="https://lucene.apache.org/core/3_5_0/api/all/org/apache/lucene/search/NGramPhraseQuery.html" rel="nofollow">NGramPhraseQuery</a> using Elasticsearch.</p>&#xA;
<p>Please can you tell me what exactly you want to know??I think you are looking forward to use Ngram Analyzer in Elasticsearch.</p>&#xA;&#xA;<p>Step1.Put your analyzer to respected index.</p>&#xA;&#xA;<pre><code>    curl -XPUT 'localhost:9200/test' -d '&#xA;{&#xA;    "settings" : {&#xA;        "analysis" : {&#xA;            "analyzer" : {&#xA;                "my_ngram_analyzer" : {&#xA;                    "tokenizer" : "my_ngram_tokenizer"&#xA;                }&#xA;            },&#xA;            "tokenizer" : {&#xA;                "my_ngram_tokenizer" : {&#xA;                    "type" : "nGram",&#xA;                    "min_gram" : "2",&#xA;                    "max_gram" : "3",&#xA;                    "token_chars": [ "letter", "digit" ]&#xA;                }&#xA;            }&#xA;        }&#xA;    }&#xA;}'&#xA;&#xA;curl 'localhost:9200/test/_analyze?       pretty=1&amp;analyzer=my_ngram_analyzer' -d 'FC Schalke 04'&#xA;         # FC, Sc, Sch, ch, cha, ha, hal, al, alk, lk, lke, ke, 04&#xA;</code></pre>&#xA;&#xA;<p>It will break your text in provided min_gram &amp; max_gram.In this case it is 2,3 respectively.</p>&#xA;
