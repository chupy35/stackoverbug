Documentation on the _linky_ filter
<http://docs.angularjs.org/api/angular.module.ngSanitize.filter.linky>

You need to load and include the "sanitize" module to use "linky":

Look for these two things: 1\. I added "<http://docs.angularjs.org/angular-
sanitize-1.0.0rc10.min.js>" as a resource 2\. I linked it into my module by
adding "ngSanitize" to angular.module('myApp',['ngSanitize']);

See here: <http://jsfiddle.net/johnlindquist/y2eJL/>
