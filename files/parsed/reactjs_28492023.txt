Update:

In the newer versions it is
([documentation](https://facebook.github.io/react/blog/2015/10/01/react-
render-and-top-level-api.html))

    
    
    ReactDOM.render(reactElement, domContainerNode)
    

* * *

It is should be `React.render` and not `React.renderComponent` as per
[documentation](http://facebook.github.io/react/docs/top-level-
api.html#react.render)

The video you mentioned uses 0.8 and you are using 0.13.

From 0.12 `renderComponent` has been replaced with `render`
