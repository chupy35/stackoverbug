When `"default_field"` is not specified in the query, elasticsearch is using
special `[_all](http://www.elasticsearch.org/guide/reference/mapping/all-
field.html)` field to perform the search. Since you didn't change the default
analyzer nor specified an analyzer for the `_all` field in your mapping,
searches against the `_all` field are performed using
[standard](http://www.elasticsearch.org/guide/reference/index-
modules/analysis/standard-analyzer.html) analyzer.
